This Weeks WCCO Animal Humane Society Spotlight

Each week the Animal Humane Society and WCCO come together to spotlight pets that are having a hard time finding a home.

Chester

  • Spaid Female
  • 3 years old
  • 11 pounds
  • Adoption Fee: $130

My caretaker passed away, so I came to Animal Humane Society to find a new home.

I’ll be a loving companion, but it may take me a little longer to acclimate to new people and new environments. A quieter, low-activity home would allow me the opportunity to get used to new things at my own pace.

I love to explore my surroundings and may need plenty of toys and activity to keep me busy and engaged. I probably won't demand too many cuddles or kisses — I just want to hang out with you and be a part of your daily activities. Families with older children may be especially suitable for cats like me.
